Four years. Countless rehearsals. One extraordinary ensemble.

The families of the Duke Ellington School of the Arts Theatre Ensemble are gathering to celebrate our Class of 2026 as they take their final bow at Ellington and step boldly into their next act on campuses and stages across the country.

This is a community celebration, hosted by our Ensemble families, for our Ensemble families. Come in your college gear, bring a dish to share, and let's send our scholars off the right way with great food, good people, and a rooftop view.
